For projects in Data 601 you pick the data source for your project​ from a list I provide (see Blackboard > Course Materials > list of data sources), or you provide a data source in your proposal.​ Data discovery is outside the scope of this class, but you're not restricted to picking from the list I provide. You should pick a data source you have some background knowledge in​. Do not use a data source for which existing analysis can be found. Data should be tabular and have at least 5 columns and at least 100 rows. Data should not exceed 100MB in size. Data should be in a single file. No preprocessing of data should be done outside of your Jupyter notebook. Data should be text (not images or audio or video).
