4: checksums **************************************

Lastly, a tip on ensuring a file hasn't changed since this check was previous performed.

A checksum is the concept that the contents of a file can be validated to not have changed.

I use MD5 sums for files that I want to confirm are unaltered. ​

import hashlib

md5 = lambda x: hashlib.md5(open(x, ‘rb’).read()).hexdigest()
